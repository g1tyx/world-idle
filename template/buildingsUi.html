If you hold down SHIFT key:
* you can buy 10 buildings with a single click.
* you can change worker distribution by 10 with a single click

If you hold down CTRL key:
* you can buy 100 buildings with a single click.
* you can change worker distribution by 100 with a single click

Who does the work?
Every male between age 18 and 65.
60% of women between age 18 and 65.

How are people distributed among workplaces?
People are distributed between buildings starting from the first. If all work places filled for the first building, second building work places will start to get filled.
If you don't want to fill all workplaces for certain building, change values by clicking + or - buttons on the building row. (You have to buy an upgrade to see + or - buttons)
